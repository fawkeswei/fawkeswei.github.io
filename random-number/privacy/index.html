Privacy Policy for Random Number - RNG Toolkit

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Random Number - RNG Toolkit ("the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand what data we collect and how we use it.

Information Collection and Use

Random Number - RNG Toolkit is designed to be a simple, privacy-focused random number generator app. Your preferences and history are stored locally on your device.

The App collects and uses the following information:

  • App Preferences: Your preferred random number ranges and algorithm settings are stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Number History: The history of generated random numbers is stored locally on your device for your convenience. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

Data Storage

All app data including preferences and number history is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that collect or store any of your data.

Third-Party Services

Random Number - RNG Toolkit does not use any third-party analytics, advertising services, or external data collection services. The app functions entirely offline and locally on your device.

Permissions

The App does not request any special device permissions beyond basic app functionality. No camera, microphone, location, or network access is required or requested.

Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers. We do not have servers that store your personal data.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The app functions entirely locally on your device without any data transmission.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the history within the app settings
  • Resetting preferences within the app
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
